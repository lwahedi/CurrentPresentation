  • Unraveling: Vulnerability from partner's partners'
  • Saturation: Non-linear transaction costs

The Model

  1. Tie Formation
  2. Strategic Interaction
  3. Counter Terrorist
  4. Collection

Tie Formation

  1. Each group randomly encounters others
  2. Estimates expected utility of partnerships, given:
    • Risk of unraveling
    • Transaction (saturation) costs
    • Past defections
  3. Form best tie with encountered groups
  4. If out of resources, break least valuable tie

Strategic Interaction

  1. For all defections at t-1, all mutual partners pick sides and inactivate tie for round
  2. For each tie, evaluate expected utility of cooperation/defection
  3. Defecting groups steal a sum from cooperating groups

Counter Terrorist

  1. Randomly select group to target
  2. From groups targeted in previous round, add their partners to target list with probability inversely correlated to resources
  3. Impose cost on random selection of targeted groups, subject to constrained resources.
